Call For Papers

International Conference On Innovations in Electronics & Communication Engineering


Papers are invited for presentation and publication in proceedings of the conference in the  above areas/fields/discipines. Best peer reviewed papers will be published in the “Journal of Innovations in Electronics and Communication (JICE)” and “Journal of Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering (JiCSE)”.
Best papers will be published in the conference proceedings with the valid ISBN number.The conference will provide an interactive platform for these personnel, participants and other delegates from the academia and industry.It would facilitate the scientific research from academic institutions, defence and industry segments across India and globe to share their research expertise, experiment breakthroughs or vision/trends in the following broad areas.

Conference Topics
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Bio Medical Signal processing
  • Image Processing
  • VLSI Design
  • Radars & Microwaves
  • Mobile Communication
  • Anteena and Wave Propagation
  • Satellite and Tv Engineering
  • Automation and Controls
  • Robotics
  • Sensors and BioMetrics
  • Microprocessors and Embedded Systems
  • Defence/Nuclear Electronics
  • Electronic Devices and Circuits
  • Mechatronics
